My name is Davey Perron and I am a composer for film and video games, most known for my work in the metal band Ultra Nightmare and the synthwave band NeoNeoN. I specialize in darker themes, running the gamut from industrial, epic and heavy to haunting, somber and melancholic. A fast-paced workflow allows me to prioritize quality above all-else, never afraid to discard ideas that don't work and iterate quickly on the ones that do.
